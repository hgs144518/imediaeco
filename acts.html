Solar Drum

Solar Drum is a pop band originally from London. They formed their band in 2016 and has been big since then. They had hits with "Wasted" (2017) and "Okay then" (2018).

DJ John Alfred

DJ John Alfred is a Canadian hip hop/rap producer and DJ from. He has had releases on record labels such as Ministry of Sound, Deconstruction Records, Logic Records, and 3 Beat Music. He also owns and runs Joof Recordings.

Everyone's Environment

Everyone's Environment is an British indie band formed in Harrogate in 2010 by members Brandon Wilson (lead vocals, bass) and Dave Johnson (lead guitar, backing vocals). Mark Smith (rhythm guitar, backing vocals) and Ron Newton (drums)

Green Gizmos

Green Gizmos is six piece act from York, formed in 2017. Since then they have released 2 albums. They use puppets and songs to engage children.
