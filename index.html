Recycling Initiatives

 

Eco Fest is a family friendly, one day festival on the Stray in Harrogate. All the money raised will go to Save All Green Spaces, a local charity. They work to preserve green spaces like Stray and keep them free of littering and pollution.

The festival will include live music from many amazing acts and good facilities.
